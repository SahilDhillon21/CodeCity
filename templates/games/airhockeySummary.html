1. The player sitting on left has to use the left hand and the player sitting on right has to use the right hand.
2. Move your hand and try to hit the puck. Make sure your other hand is not distinctively visible.
3. Once the puck crosses the vertical boundary of either side, the opposing player wins! Each touch counts as a point.
4. Press 'r' to continue playing, and 'q' to quit.
